# FAQ Section

## What are AI glasses?

AI glasses are wearable devices that combine eyewear with artificial intelligence features such as voice assistants, cameras, translations, navigation, and information retrieval.

## How do AI glasses work?

They use cameras, microphones, sensors, speakers, and AI software to understand surroundings and assist users through voice and visual interactions.

## Are AI glasses the same as AR glasses?

No. AI glasses focus on intelligent assistance, while AR glasses primarily overlay digital information onto the real world.

## Can AI glasses translate languages?

Many modern AI glasses offer real-time translation capabilities for conversations and text.

## Are AI glasses worth buying?

They can be valuable for travelers, professionals, content creators, and technology enthusiasts seeking hands-free access to AI tools.

## What are the best AI glasses?

Popular options include the Ray-Ban Meta Smart Glasses, Xreal devices, and other emerging AI-powered smart eyewear products.
